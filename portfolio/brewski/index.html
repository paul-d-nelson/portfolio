The Best Beer In The West

Welcome to Brewski Manly Micro-Brews. Our mission is simple: produce the best, most flavorful beers using the finest traditional ingredients with the best of both old and new brewing techniques.

Featured Beers

  • Oktoberfest

    Our manly version of this German classic blends hearty malts for a deep, smooth caramel flavor that’s perfect for fall.

  • I.P.A

    Get ready for a burly bite: hopped for a sharp, clean, citrus flavor with just a hint of mellow malt. Audacious from start to finish.

  • Hefweizen

    Our true to style take on the classic, unfiltered, Bavarian wheat ales. Crisp and cloudy the way real men take a “Hefe.

  • Belgian White

    Smooth and spicy. We shook up the traditional Belgian-style wheat with a uniquely-crafted mix of manly citrus spices.
